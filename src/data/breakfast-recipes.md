## 🥚 RECIPE B-001: Sous Vide Egg Bites (Starbucks Copycat)

**Category:** Breakfast | **Type:** Main
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Servings:** 6 jars

### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 1 hour (Sous Vide)
- **Storage:** 1 week fridge

### Ingredients

- 6 large eggs
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese
- 1/2 cup shredded Gruyere or Cheddar
- 1/4 cup cooked bacon bits
- Salt and pepper
- 6 small mason jars (4oz)

**Spice Ratios:**

- **Gruyere/Bacon (Dominant):** Salty Umami
- **Cottage Cheese (Carrier):** Creamy Base
- **Pepper (Accent):** Bite

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Blend:** Blend eggs, cottage cheese, salt, pepper until smooth.
2. **Fill:** Place bacon/cheese in jars. Pour egg mix over. Finger-tighten lids.
3. **Seal:** Vacuum seal the JARS (using jar attachment or chamber sealer). Or just cook in water bath.
4. **Cook:** Sous vide at 172°F (78°C) for 1 hour.
5. **Store:** Keep sealed jars in fridge. Reheat in microwave.

### Pro Tips

- **[TEXTURE] Cottage Cheese Blitz:** The secret to Starbucks-style velvet texture is blending *Cottage Cheese* and *Gruyère* into the eggs. Milk alone makes them rubbery.
- **[GAS WARNING] Mason Jar Physics:** **Critical:** Finger tight only! If you crank the lid shut, air cannot escape during cooking and the jar will explode.
- **Texture:** The vacuum/sous vide method creates a velvety, custard-like texture.

## 🥤 RECIPE B-002: Smoothie Packs

**Category:** Breakfast | **Type:** Drink
**Difficulty:** Easy | **Servings:** 10 packs

### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 20 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 0
- **Storage:** 6 months frozen

### Ingredients

- Bananas, sliced
- Berries (strawberries, blueberries)
- Spinach or Kale
- Protein powder (optional)
- Chia seeds

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Prep:** Slice all fruit.
2. **Portion:** Place one serving of fruit/veg/seeds in a bag.
3. **Seal:** Vacuum seal. **Pulse** mode if fruit is soft.
4. **Freeze:** Store flat.
5. **Use:** Dump frozen block into blender, add liquid (milk/water), blend.

### Pro Tips

- **[PRESERVATION] Lemon Bath:** Toss bananas/apples in lemon water before sealing to keep them bright white.
- **[NUTRITION] Spinach Pucks:** Blend spinach with water and freeze in cubes, then seal the 'green pucks' with the fruit to avoid shattered leaves.
- **No Ice Needed:** Since the fruit is frozen solid without freezer burn, it acts as the ice.

## 🥣 RECIPE B-003: Overnight Oats Packs

**Category:** Breakfast | **Type:** Main
**Difficulty:** Easy | **Servings:** 5 packs

### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 0
- **Storage:** 3 months pantry (dry)

### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp dried fruit (raisins, cranberries)
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 scoop protein powder (optional)

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Mix:** Combine dry ingredients.
2. **Seal:** Vacuum seal in small bags.
3. **Use:** Cut bag, dump into jar, add milk/yogurt, refrigerate overnight.

### Pro Tips

- **[FLAVOR] Toast the Oats:** Vacuum sealing raw oats traps cardboard flavor. **Mandatory:** Toast dry oats in butter for 3 mins before bagging to release nutty aroma.
- **[ADD-IN] Frozen Fruit:** Use *frozen* berries. They release juice as they thaw in the bag, creating natural syrup.
- **Travel Ready:** Perfect for camping or travel.

## 🥓 RECIPE B-004: Homemade Breakfast Sausage

**Category:** Breakfast | **Type:** Protein
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Servings:** 12 patties

### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 20 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 10 minutes
- **Storage:** 6 months frozen

### Ingredients

- 1 lb ground pork
- 1 tsp sage
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

**Spice Ratios:**

- **Sage/Thyme (Dominant):** Classic Breakfast Sausage
- **Nutmeg/Sugar (Supporting):** Warm Sweetness
- **Red Pepper (Accent):** Mild Heat

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Mix:** Combine pork and spices. Mix well.
2. **Shape:** Form into patties.
3. **Freeze:** Flash freeze on tray for 1 hour.
4. **Seal:** Vacuum seal frozen patties.
5. **Cook:** Pan fry from frozen or thawed.

### Pro Tips

- **Flavor:** Vacuum sealing melds the sage and nutmeg into the pork for superior flavor.

## 🥗 RECIPE B-005: Vacuum-Infused Fruit Salad

**Category:** Breakfast | **Type:** Side
**Difficulty:** Easy | **Servings:** 4

### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Marination:** 15 mins
- **Storage:** 3 days fridge

### Ingredients

- Melon cubes (cantaloupe, honeydew)
- Pineapple chunks
- Fresh mint leaves
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp lime juice

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Combine:** Toss fruit with honey, lime, mint.
2. **Seal:** Vacuum seal on **High**.
3. **Wait:** 15 minutes. The fruit will become translucent and dense.
4. **Serve:** Immediately or chill.

### Pro Tips

- **Science:** The vacuum breaks cell walls, making the fruit juicy and dense, almost like it was poached.
