   - Roast from frozen until golden

**Preparation Timeline:**

- Day 1: Seal ribeye for aging, prepare and freeze sides/butter
- Day 21: Remove steak, bring to room temp, sear in cast iron
- Cook sides while steak rests

**The Vacuum Advantage:** The 21-day wet aging creates a depth of flavor and tenderness that simply cannot be replicated without vacuum sealing. Standard refrigerator storage would spoil the meat.
