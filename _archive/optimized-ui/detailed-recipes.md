#### Step-by-Step Instructions

**PREPARATION:**

1. **Prepare Work Surface**
   - Clear a clean work area
   - Have vacuum bags ready
   - Cut parchment paper into 4" x 4" squares (8 pieces)

2. **Mix Seasonings**
   - In a large bowl, combine ground beef with all seasonings
   - Mix gently with hands for 30-60 seconds
   - Do NOT overmix - this makes burgers tough
   - The meat should look evenly seasoned but still loose

3. **Portion the Meat**
   - Divide seasoned beef into 8 equal portions (62.5g / 2.2 oz each)
   - Gently form each portion into a loose ball
   - Do NOT compress - keep them loose for better texture

4. **Shape for Vacuum Sealing**

   - Keep edges loose and irregular
   - The vacuum will compress them slightly

**VACUUM SEALING:**

5. **Layer the Patties**

   - Add parchment square on top
   - Add second patty
   - Repeat layering (parchment between each patty)
   - You can seal 2-4 patties per bag depending on bag size

6. **Seal the Bags**
   - Place bag in vacuum sealer
   - Use GENTLE or DELICATE setting if available (prevents over-compression)
   - Seal the bag
   - The patties will flatten slightly - this is normal
   - Check seal is complete

7. **Label and Store**
   - Write on bag with permanent marker:
     - "Smash Burger Patties"
     - Number of patties
     - Date sealed
     - "Best by: [2-3 years from date]"
   - Place flat in freezer
   - Stack for space efficiency

**COOKING FROM FROZEN:**

8. **Thawing (Optional)**
   - Option 1: Thaw in fridge overnight (recommended)
   - Option 2: Cook from frozen (add 1-2 minutes to cook time)
   - Option 3: Quick thaw in cold water (sealed bag) for 20 minutes

9. **Cook the Smash Burgers**
   - Heat cast iron skillet or griddle to HIGH heat (400-450Â°F)
   - Lightly oil the surface
   - Place burger ball on hot surface
   - IMMEDIATELY smash hard with sturdy spatula for 3-5 seconds
   - Press until 1/4" thin - you want maximum contact
   - Cook 2 minutes until deep brown crust forms
   - Flip, add cheese, cook 1-2 minutes more
   - Internal temp should reach 160Â°F (71Â°C)

10. **Serve**

    - Add toppings as desired
    - Serve immediately while crust is crispy

**Vacuum Sealed:**

- **Refrigerator:** 7-10 days (if planning to use soon)

- Use a sturdy spatula - cheap ones won't apply enough pressure
- The thinner you smash, the better the crust-to-meat ratio

**Seasoning Science:**

- Garlic and onion powders > fresh for vacuum sealing (no moisture, no botulism risk)
- Smoked paprika adds umami depth without overpowering beef flavor

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

**PREPARATION:**

   - Place grated onion in paper towel and squeeze out excess liquid
   - Reserve onion (moisture removed)
   - Finely chop parsley and mint
   - Mince or grate garlic

2. **Mix the Kofte Base**
   - In large bowl, combine ground chicken
   - Add squeezed grated onion
   - Add garlic, parsley, mint
   - Add all spices: cumin, coriander, paprika, pepper, salt, red pepper flakes, cinnamon
   - Add breadcrumbs
   - Add egg
   - Add olive oil

   - Using hands (best) or wooden spoon, mix for 2-3 minutes

   - Mixture should be well combined and uniform

4. **Test Seasoning (Optional but Recommended)**

   - Cook in skillet 2-3 minutes per side

   - They'll hold shape better during sealing

**VACUUM SEALING:**

7. **Prepare for Sealing**

   - Remove koftas from fridge

8. **Layer in Vacuum Bags**

   - Put parchment paper between each layer
   - Arrange in single layer if possible
   - Leave 3" at top of bag for sealing

9. **Gentle Vacuum Seal**
   - Use GENTLE or DELICATE setting if available
   - If no gentle setting, use PULSE mode
   - You want to remove air WITHOUT crushing the koftas
   - Pulse several times until air is mostly out
   - Seal the bag
   - The koftas may compress slightly - this is acceptable

      - "Chicken Kofte"
      - "16-20 pieces"
      - Date sealed
      - "Cook from frozen or thawed"
      - "Best by: [1-2 years from date]"

    - Once frozen solid, can stack vertically

**COOKING FROM FROZEN OR THAWED:**
