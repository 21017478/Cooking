### ğŸ‰ RECIPE D-001: Compressed Watermelon with Mint

**Category:** Dessert | **Type:** Fruit
**Difficulty:** Easy | **Servings:** 4

#### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Marination:** 30 mins (vacuum)
- **Storage:** 2 days fridge

#### Ingredients

- 1/4 watermelon, cubed (seedless)
- 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp sugar (optional)

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Bag:** Place watermelon cubes in bag. Add mint and lime.
2. **Seal:** Vacuum seal on **High**.
3. **Wait:** Refrigerate 30 minutes.
4. **Serve:** The watermelon will turn deep red and translucent. The texture becomes meaty and dense.

#### Pro Tips

- **Fancy:** Serve with feta cheese for a sweet/salty appetizer.

### ğŸ RECIPE D-002: Boozy Pineapple (Rum Infused)

**Category:** Dessert | **Type:** Fruit
**Difficulty:** Easy | **Servings:** 4

#### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Marination:** 30 mins
- **Storage:** 1 week fridge

#### Ingredients

- 1 pineapple, peeled and cored, cut into spears
- 1/2 cup dark rum
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 cinnamon stick

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Combine:** Place pineapple and liquids in bag.
2. **Seal:** Vacuum seal on **Pulse** (careful with liquid).
3. **Marinate:** 30 minutes. The rum penetrates the fruit completely.
4. **Serve:** Grill the spears or serve cold over ice cream.

#### Pro Tips

- **Instant Infusion:** Tastes like it soaked for days.

**Category:** Dessert | **Type:** Main
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Servings:** 6 jars

#### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 20 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 90 minutes
- **Storage:** 1 week fridge

#### Ingredients

- 8oz cream cheese, softened
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp vanilla
- Graham cracker crumbs (for bottom)

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Crust:** Press crumbs into bottom of small mason jars.
2. **Filling:** Blend cheese, sugar, egg, cream, vanilla. Pour over crust.
3. **Seal:** Finger-tighten lids. Vacuum seal jars (optional but helps).
4. **Cook:** Sous vide at 176Â°F (80Â°C) for 90 minutes.
5. **Chill:** Refrigerate overnight.

#### Pro Tips

- **Perfect Texture:** Never cracks, always creamy.

### ğŸ« RECIPE D-004: Chocolate Lava Cakes (Prep & Freeze)

**Category:** Dessert | **Type:** Main
**Difficulty:** Hard | **Servings:** 4

#### Time Requirements

- **Prep Time:** 20 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 12 minutes
- **Storage:** 3 months frozen

#### Ingredients

- 4oz dark chocolate
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 eggs + 2 yolks
- 6 tbsp flour

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Melt:** Melt chocolate and butter. Whisk in sugar, eggs, flour.
2. **Fill:** Pour into buttered ramekins.
3. **Freeze:** Freeze ramekins until solid (2 hours).
4. **Seal:** Pop frozen pucks out of ramekins. Vacuum seal the pucks.
5. **Cook:** Place frozen puck back in ramekin. Bake at 425Â°F (220Â°C) for 12-14 mins.

#### Pro Tips

- **Dinner Party:** Prep weeks ahead, bake fresh for guests.
