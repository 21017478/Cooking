Vacuum Sealing Storage Times

These are approximate maximum storage times for vacuum-sealed foods in the freezer:

  • Ground Beef/Pork: 2-3 years (vs 6 months unsealed)
  • Chicken/Turkey: 1-2 years (vs 6-12 months unsealed)
  • Fish/Seafood: 1-2 years (vs 6-8 months unsealed)
  • Cooked Meals: 6-12 months (vs 3-6 months unsealed)
  • Vegetables (blanched): 12-18 months (vs 8-10 months unsealed)
  • Hard Cheeses: 4-8 months frozen (vs 2-3 months unsealed)
