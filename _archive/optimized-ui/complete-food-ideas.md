## STORAGE TIPS FOR SIDES

1. **Vegetables:** Always blanch cruciferous vegetables before sealing
2. **Starches:** Cool completely before sealing to prevent moisture
3. **Sauces:** Use pulse function or pre-freeze to prevent liquid extraction
4. **Compound Butters:** Roll into logs, slice portions as needed
5. **Bread:** Seal in portions you'll use at once (resealing affects quality)
6. **Beans:** Seal with minimal liquid for best texture upon reheating
