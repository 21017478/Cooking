## ğŸ’ BAGS & ROLLS 101: Everything You Need to Know

### **1. Why "Textured" Bags are REQUIRED**

- **The Problem:** Smooth bags (like Ziplocs) trap air because the plastic sticks together.
* **The Solution:** Textured bags have tiny bumps that create "air tunnels" so the machine can suck the air out.
* **Rule:** If it doesn't have bumps, it won't work.

### **2. Rolls vs. Pre-Cut Bags (The Math)**

- **Pre-Cut Bags:** Convenient but expensive (~Â£0.20 per bag).
* **Rolls:** You cut exactly what you need.
  * *Example:* Sealing a single sausage? Cut a 10cm strip.
  * *Example:* Sealing a whole rack of ribs? Cut a 40cm strip.
  * **Savings:** Rolls are ~30-50% cheaper per meter than pre-cut bags.
